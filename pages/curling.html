Ice Curling

I first got into curling back in 2010, when China’s women’s team won the bronze medal at the Vancouver Winter Olympics. I started watching it on TV every now and then after that and was instantly intrigued. 11 years later, I finally got the chance to try it myself! I joined the MIT Curling Club in the winter of 2021 and picked it up pretty quickly. After a few months of training, I was very honored to be selected to represent MIT in the 2022 College National Championship held in Fargo, North Dakota.
