Flying as an FAA Certified Private Pilot

As an aviation enthusiast, I have been dreaming of flying an airplane since my early teenage. My first attempt of becoming a pilot took place when I was 15 (on Microsoft Flight Simulator X), and since then I had accumulated around 700 "sim hours" before I started my Private Pilot training in Feb 2023.

My base airport is Peachtree DeKalb County Airport (KPDK), located in the northeast suburb of Atlanta.

Training Timeline

  • First Flight -- 2023-Feb-19
  • First Solo Flight -- 2023-Aug-13 @ 35 flight hrs
  • Private Pilot Certification -- 2024-May-29 @ 93 flight hrs
